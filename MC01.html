Ongoing




Two homes on a hill.

The first home nestles into the hillside, enjoying the security of earth to one side, and the prospect of unencumbered space to the other.

This structure is predominantly uniform - it is the product of natural limitations imposed by the site itself: chiefly the topography. The slope (a 12% grade) creates a variety of conditions that must be addressed while simultaneously balancing the associated budgetary constraints.
